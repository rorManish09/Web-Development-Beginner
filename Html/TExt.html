The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had beeen starting out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all the other scientist journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college, an the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she neeeded to be free.

She had spent the last tenn years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do. Althouth many considered her pioneering work, unravelling the secrets of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievemant, Molly didthink she had something better to do.
