A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

The Briards or berger de brie, is larger breed of dog tridationally used a aherder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as senteies, messengers and to search for wounded soilders because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in First World War almost to the point of extinciton. Currebtky the population of briards is slowly recovering. Charlemange, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson and Lafayetta all owned briards.
